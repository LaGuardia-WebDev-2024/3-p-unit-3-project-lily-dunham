Fairy Bread

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Slice of bread 1
butter one knife's worth
butter knife 1
sprinkles 3 teaspoons

Step 1: Put your piece of bread in the toaster.
Step 2: Take your bread out of the toaster and cover one side with butter
Step 3: Cover your bread with sprinkles
Step 4: Enjoy!
