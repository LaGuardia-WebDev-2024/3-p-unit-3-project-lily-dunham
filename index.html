Fairy Bread

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Slice of bread 1
butter one knife's worth
butter knife 1
sprinkles 3 teaspoons

Step 1: Put your piece of bread in the toaster.
Step 2: Take your bread out of the toaster and cover one side with butter
Step 3: Cover your bread with sprinkles
Step 4: Enjoy!

French Fries

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
potato 2
olive oil 1/4 cup
salt 1 teaspoon

Step 1: slice your potato into tiny fry shapes.
Step 2: put oil into a pan and let it rise to a boil
Step 3: put the french fries into the boiling oil and let them fry until the outsides are crispy
Step 4: take the french fries out and dab them in a paper towel to get excess oil off, then cover them in salt and shake well. Enjoy!
